About
This site serves as my academic homepage, hosting information about my research, publications, professional background, and blog.
Research focus: Privacy-preserving mechanisms in Vertical Federated Learning (VFL) systems, with particular interest in the fusion of Adversarial Training, Knowledge Distillation, and Differential Privacy as a defense against adversarial inference attacks.
